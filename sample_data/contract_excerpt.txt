SERVICES AGREEMENT (EXCERPT)

This Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2026 ("Effective Date"),
by and between:

Northbridge Consulting Group, Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of
business at 88 Liberty Street, New York, NY 10006 ("Provider"),

and

Cascade Retail Holdings, LLC, an Oregon limited liability company with its principal place of
business at 4100 SW Macadam Ave, Portland, OR 97239 ("Client").

1. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of
twelve (12) months, expiring on February 28, 2027, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.
The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month terms unless either party
provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the
then-current term.

2. SERVICES AND OBLIGATIONS
Provider shall deliver quarterly operational audits, a monthly performance dashboard, and
on-call advisory support not to exceed 10 hours per month. Client shall provide timely access
to relevant financial records, designate a single point of contact, and remit payment within
fifteen (15) days of invoice receipt.

3. COMPENSATION
Client shall pay Provider a fixed monthly retainer of $7,500, invoiced on the first business
day of each month. Any work exceeding the included advisory hours shall be billed at $225/hour.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days written
notice if the breach remains uncured. Client may terminate for convenience upon ninety (90)
days written notice.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed during
the term of this Agreement and for a period of three (3) years following termination.
